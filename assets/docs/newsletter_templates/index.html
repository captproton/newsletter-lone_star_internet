Newsletter Listing

This screen is used to manage newsletters. You can Create, Edit, Publish, Unpublish and reorder newsletters from this screen. A link to the newsletter archive is also provided.

  • Click "New Newsletter" in the navigation to create a newsletter
  • Click "Edit" next to the newsletter you would like to manage/modify.
  • Click "Publish" on a newsletter for it to show up in the archive
  • Click "Unpublish" to remove a newsletter from the archive
  • Click on the name of a newsletter and drag it up or down to reorder them in the archive
  • Click "View Newsletter Archive" to open up your archive in a new window
  • Click "Delete" to delete the newsletter.

New Newsletter

This screen is used to add a new newsletter. Give the newsletter a name that is descriptive as it will show up in your archive listing with this value. Choose a design for your newsletter at this time. You cannot change this after you have created a newsletter; if you want to use a different design, you can delete the unwanted newsletter and start a new one. If no newsletter designs exist, or you would like a new one added, contact your site administrator.

Newsletter Archive

The archive is simply a list of newsletters that you have published. Their order and whether they are published is controlled on the Newsletter Listing screen.

Newsletter Designs

These are provided by your site administrator, who can install and modify them to achieve different looks for your newsletter. Newsletter designs are made up of the following items.

  • Newsletter Areas are sections of your newsletter which can contain different "Newsletter Elements". Examples of these can be headers, footers or columns in your article.
  • "Newsletter Elements" are things which the designer of your design has provided for you to add content to your newsletter, such as images and articles, and provide a layout and style for the content.
